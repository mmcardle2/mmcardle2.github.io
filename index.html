My Roommate's Cat Oliver

Who is Oliver?

Physical Description

Oliver is a very cute short/medium haired tuxedo cat with brown and green eyes. His pupils dilate or constrict with the brightness of the room unless he's in a playful mood, then his pupils will be very dilated and his eyes open as wide as they can go. He has white 'socks' on all four of his feet and a white stomach. Oliver has very long whiskers, both to the sides of his mouth/nose and above his eyes like eyebrows.

Where He's From

My roommate adopted him from a foster parent who had several dogs, cats, and birds. He was adopted when he was 6 months old. We think his busy and crowded childhood is what influenced him to be such an unusually social and easy-going cat. He was very tiny when we got him, but he's really grown into his size.

Oliver's Future

My roommate and I are living together for this school year and the next, after which we will both graduate. She will stay in Madison with Oliver and go to grad school, while I will likely move for my career, leaving Oliver behind. It may be difficult for him, with his outgoing personality, to go from four people in the house to only one, but by then he will be older and less active.
